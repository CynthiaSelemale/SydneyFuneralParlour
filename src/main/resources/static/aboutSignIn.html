Our Story

In 2012, Sydney Mokwena founded Sydney's Funeral Advisors and Services with a heartfelt mission: to be a comforting beacon for families navigating loss. With a blend of empathy and expertise, Sydney envisioned a sanctuary where grieving families could find solace and support. At Sydney's, our values—integrity, compassion, and excellence—are more than words; they're the guiding lights of our service. Each family we serve is treated with the utmost respect and dignity, their journey honored, and their loved one's legacy celebrated. From traditional ceremonies to personalized cremation services and thoughtful pre-planning, we offer a comprehensive range of options tailored to every family's needs and preferences. As a family-owned business, we take pride in providing personalized care, treating each client as an extension of our own family. But our commitment extends beyond the funeral process; we're active members of our community, supporting initiatives that uplift and enrich lives. We're deeply grateful for the trust families place in us during their most challenging moments. It's an honor to walk alongside them, and we're dedicated to upholding the highest standards of service and compassion in everything we do.
