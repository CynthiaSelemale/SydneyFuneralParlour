Funeral Plans & Advise

Family Plan

  • 4 Package Plans

Terms & Conditions

For all plans there is a joining fee of R100 Monthly premiums are unique to the plans. The plans cover 10 people plus the policy holder/ member. There are no restrictions to the people covered; it can be any person a policy holder wishes to cover between ages 0 - 65 years. Waiting period of 6 months, should there be any natural causes death within the 6 months waiting period SFAS will offer an alternative advice. Death due accidents will be covered immediately. Service will be rendered within 80 km. Service for all plans includes undertaking services and administration.
